# 高考英语听力讲评：What does the man usually drink

> **课型**：听力讲评课
> **适用对象**：高三 / 高二复习
> **建议时长**：45 分钟
> **配套音频**：详情页"音频资源"区可在线收听整套听力原声

本备课稿覆盖完整一套高考听力卷的讲评思路，重点回答：

- 每道题的"抓分关键"在哪一句
- 学生最容易踩的三类干扰：转折否定 / 张冠李戴 / 数字常识诱导
- 一个高频语法点：`the + 形容词`
- 一份开课前 5 分钟可直接念给学生的做题策略

---

### 第一节　听 5 段短对话，每段读两遍

---

#### 第 1 段　对应 Q1

> **听力原文**
> W: I've started drinking tea recently and I'm getting into it now.
> 我最近开始喝茶了，现在越来越喜欢了。
> M: Well, coffee is still my first choice, with lots of milk and sugar. I know it's not your thing, but it works for me.
> 嗯，咖啡仍是我的首选，加很多牛奶和糖。我知道你不喜欢，但它适合我。

**Q1. What does the man usually drink?　男士通常喝什么？　答案 B**
A. Tea. 茶　B. Coffee. 咖啡　C. Juice. 果汁
- 抓分：问 the **man**。女方喝 tea 是干扰，男方用转折 "Well" 亮出 coffee。

---

#### 第 2 段　对应 Q2

> **听力原文**
> Josh: Betty, are you going to Guangzhou this winter holiday?
> Betty，你这个寒假要去广州吗？
> Betty: Yes, Josh. I'm thinking of doing some sightseeing and climbing there.
> 是的，Josh。我打算去那儿观光、爬山。
> Josh: Nice. I was considering a trip for a long time, but I haven't seen my grandparents for a long time, so I'll spend some time with them.
> 不错。我也想去旅行很久了，但我很久没见祖父母了，所以打算陪陪他们。

**Q2. What will Josh probably do during the winter holiday?　Josh 寒假可能会做什么？　答案 C**
A. Climb mountains. 爬山　B. Travel to Guangzhou. 去广州旅行　C. Visit his grandparents. 看望祖父母
- 抓分：观光、爬山、去广州都是 **Betty** 的；问的是 Josh → 转折 but 后陪祖父母。

---

#### 第 3 段　对应 Q3

> **听力原文**
> W: Oh, no. I left my phone at home. This is terrible for the journey.
> 糟了，我把手机落在家里了。这趟出行可糟糕了。
> M: Don't worry about it. We're not that far yet. Let's just turn around and pick it up quickly.
> 别担心，我们还没走多远。咱们掉头快点回去拿吧。

**Q3. What does the man suggest doing?　男士建议做什么？　答案 C**
A. Calling off the trip. 取消行程　B. Asking someone for help. 找人帮忙　C. Going back for the phone. 回去拿手机
- 抓分：turn around and pick it up = 回去拿手机。

---

#### 第 4 段　对应 Q4 ★

> **听力原文**
> W: Eric, should I wear this hat for this afternoon's party?
> Eric，今天下午的派对我该戴这顶帽子吗？
> Eric: Honestly, you look great without it. There's no sun out today, and you don't need it for protection.
> 说实话，你不戴也很好看。今天没太阳，你也不需要它防晒。
> W: Yeah, but everyone will dress well today. I don't want to look out of style.
> 是啊，但今天大家都会穿得很体面，我不想显得过时。

**Q4. Why does the woman want to wear the hat?　女士为什么想戴这顶帽子？　答案 A**
A. To be fashionable. 为了时髦　B. To match her dress. 为了配裙子　C. To avoid the sunlight. 为了防晒
- 抓分：男方"今天没太阳、不用防晒"是**否定 C**；女方"不想显得过时" → 为了时髦 → A。
- 板书：`in style 时髦 ↔ out of style 过时`。

---

#### 第 5 段　对应 Q5

> **听力原文**
> M: Eric's birthday is coming up. Have you thought about what to get her?
> Eric 的生日快到了。你想好送她什么了吗？
> W: How about a dog? She's always loved animals.
> 送只狗怎么样？她一直很喜欢动物。
> M: Hmm, I'm not so sure about that. I heard her mother easily gets sick because of pet hair.
> 嗯，我不太确定。我听说她妈妈很容易因宠物毛发而生病。
> W: Oh, you're right. I should think of something else.
> 哦，你说得对。我该换个别的。

**Q5. What are the speakers mainly talking about?　说话者主要在谈论什么？　答案 B**
A. A pet. 一只宠物　B. A gift. 一份礼物　C. A family. 一个家庭
- 抓分：通篇在商量"送什么生日礼物"，dog/pet（A）只是一个被否掉的方案。

### 第二节　听 5 段对话/独白，每段读两遍

---

#### 第 6 段　对应 Q6–7

> **听力原文**
> A: Hey, Judy. What are you working on?
> 嘿，Judy，你在忙什么呢？
> Judy: Oh, just making some little paper boats and flowers for the class festival this Thursday.
> 哦，就是在做些小纸船和纸花，为这周四的班级节日做准备。
> A: Nice. As for my class, we will focus on performances this year. My brother will join me in a dance.
> 不错。我们班今年主打表演，我弟弟会和我一起跳舞。
> Judy: Sounds amazing. It's really a fun tradition at our school.
> 听起来真棒。这真是我们学校一个有趣的传统。
> A: Yeah. Want me to help you with the artwork?
> 是啊。要我帮你做手工吗？
> Judy: That'd be wonderful. Let's make them together.
> 那太好了，我们一起做吧。

**Q6. What is Judy busy doing?　Judy 正忙着做什么？　答案 B**
A. Painting pictures. 画画　B. Making paper creations. 做纸艺手工　C. Preparing for a performance. 准备表演
- 抓分：paper boats and flowers = 纸艺；performance（C）是对方班级的活动。

**Q7. What is the relationship between the speakers?　说话者之间是什么关系？　答案 A**
A. School friends. 同校同学　B. Brother and sister. 兄妹　C. Teacher and student. 师生
- 抓分：两人各属**不同班级、同一所学校**（my class / our school）→ 同学。"my brother" 是其中一人的弟弟，别误判两人是兄妹。

#### 第 7 段　对应 Q8–10

> **听力原文**
> Lisa: Hi, this is Lisa. Could I speak with Mr. Greg? I heard he's back from a business trip.
> 你好，我是 Lisa。我能和 Greg 先生通话吗？我听说他出差回来了。
> M: I'm sorry, but he felt unwell after this morning's meeting and went back for a good rest. He won't show up until tomorrow morning.
> 抱歉，他今早开完会后感觉不舒服，回去好好休息了。他要到明早才会出现。
> Lisa: Oh, that's unlucky. I need to discuss something important with him. We've made some changes to the recent orders timetable. This might affect the delivery details.
> 哦，真不巧。我有要事得和他商量。我们对近期的订单时间表做了一些改动，这可能会影响交付细节。
> M: Oh, I'll have him call you back as soon as possible. Is that okay?
> 哦，我会让他尽快回电给你，可以吗？
> Lisa: That would be great. Please make sure he has this number.
> 那太好了。请务必让他拿到这个号码。
> M: No problem. I'll let him know the message immediately.
> 没问题，我会立刻把口信转告他。
> Lisa: Thanks a lot. Have a nice day.
> 多谢。祝你愉快。

**Q8. Where is Mr. Greg most likely at the moment?　Greg 先生此刻最可能在哪里？　答案 C**
A. On a business trip. 在出差　B. In a meeting. 在开会　C. At home. 在家
- 抓分：出差（A）、开会（B）都是干扰，went back for a good rest → 在家休息 → C。

**Q9. What does Lisa need to talk to Mr. Greg about?　Lisa 需要和 Greg 谈什么？　答案 C**
A. New products. 新产品　B. Payment details. 付款细节　C. Schedule changes. 日程变动
- 抓分：changes to the orders timetable = 时间表变动 → Schedule changes。

**Q10. What is the man probably going to do next?　男士接下来可能会做什么？　答案 A**
A. Pass on the message. 转告口信　B. Review the timetable. 查看时间表　C. Check a phone number. 核对电话号码
- 抓分：他承诺的下一步动作是 "let him know the message" = 转告；timetable、number 都是干扰。

#### 第 8 段　对应 Q11–13

> **听力原文**
> Andy: Hey, Ron, a new art exhibition will be held at City Hall Center. Would you like to go?
> 嘿，Ron，市政中心要办一个新画展。你想去吗？
> Ron: Is it open to everyone, Andy?
> 它对所有人开放吗，Andy？
> Andy: Yes, it's open to the public with no tickets required, and most of the paintings are in the latest styles.
> 是的，向公众开放、无需门票，而且大多数画作都是最新风格的。
> Ron: Nice. What else can we expect?
> 不错。还有什么可期待的？
> Andy: There's photography, short films, and 3D art. The short films attract a large crowd, second only to 3D pieces.
> 有摄影、短片和 3D 艺术。短片吸引了大批人群，仅次于 3D 作品。
> Ron: That sounds cool. Can we touch any of them?
> 听起来很酷。我们能触摸其中一些吗？
> Andy: Some pieces, for sure. Just avoid the paintings. There's a digital art station where you can get hands-on experience.
> 有些作品当然可以，只是别碰画作。那里有一个数字艺术区，你可以亲手体验。
> Ron: I'm in. How about going there tomorrow afternoon?
> 我加入。明天下午去怎么样？
> Andy: No problem. You won't be disappointed.
> 没问题，你不会失望的。
> Ron: Okay. I can't wait to check everything out.
> 好的，我迫不及待想把一切都看个遍。

**Q11. What does Andy say about the new art exhibition?　关于这个新画展，Andy 怎么说？　答案 A**
A. It is free to visit. 参观免费　B. It is held online. 线上举办　C. It is in classic styles. 经典风格
- 抓分：no tickets required = 免费 → A；latest styles 与"经典"相反（排除 C）；线下办（排除 B）。

**Q12. Which part is the most popular?　哪个部分最受欢迎？　答案 B**
A. The short films. 短片　B. The 3D art. 3D 艺术　C. The photography. 摄影
- 抓分：short films **second only to 3D pieces** = 短片仅次于 3D → 3D 第一 → B。
- 对比：`second only to X` 仅次于 X｜`second to none` 无人能及。

**Q13. How does Ron feel about the art exhibition?　Ron 对这个画展感觉如何？　答案 C**
A. Calm. 平静　B. Disappointed. 失望　C. Curious. 好奇
- 抓分：I can't wait to check everything out = 迫不及待 → 好奇/感兴趣。

#### 第 9 段　对应 Q14–17

> **听力原文**
> Mr. White: Good morning, Brenda. Great to see you at the student club today. I heard you're really into volunteer work. Can you tell me more?
> 早上好，Brenda。今天很高兴在学生社团见到你。我听说你很热衷志愿工作，能多讲讲吗？
> Brenda: Nice to meet you too, Mr. White. Of course. In middle school, my classmates and I helped repair old people's homes.
> 我也很高兴见到您，White 先生。当然可以。初中时，我和同学们帮忙修缮老人的住所。
> Mr. White: That's amazing. What about in high school?
> 太了不起了。那高中呢？
> Brenda: I've mostly helped the homeless by providing meals and have volunteered at community events.
> 高中我主要是给流浪者提供饭食，还在社区活动中做志愿者。
> Mr. White: Sounds like you've done a lot. What do you like most about our club?
> 听起来你做了很多。你最喜欢我们社团哪一点？
> Brenda: Well, I love nature, and the club's rich activities are a perfect way to explore the outdoors.
> 嗯，我热爱大自然，而社团丰富的活动正是探索户外的好方式。
> Mr. White: I'm glad to hear that. You'll also enjoy our outdoor sports programs. Would you be interested in helping organize some club projects?
> 很高兴听你这么说。你也会喜欢我们的户外运动项目。你有兴趣帮忙组织一些社团项目吗？
> Brenda: I'd love to. I'm good at working with others and being creative in projects.
> 我很乐意。我擅长与人合作，也能在项目中发挥创意。
> Mr. White: That's exactly what we need. Thanks for your time today.
> 这正是我们需要的。感谢你今天抽出时间。

**Q14. What is Brenda probably doing?　Brenda 可能正在做什么？　答案 B**
A. Giving a report. 作报告　B. Taking an interview. 接受面试　C. Attending a club show. 参加社团演出
- 抓分：White 在了解她、问她社团相关问题 → 入社面谈。

**Q15. Who did Brenda help in middle school?　Brenda 初中时帮助过谁？　答案 A**
A. The elderly. 老人　B. The sick. 病人　C. The homeless. 流浪者
- 抓分：初中 = old people's homes（老人）；the homeless（流浪者）是**高中**的事（C 张冠李戴）。看清"初中/高中"。

**Q16. What about the club attracts Brenda most?　社团的哪一点最吸引 Brenda？　答案 B**
A. Its teamwork spirit. 团队精神　B. Its outdoor programs. 户外项目　C. Its community services. 社区服务
- 抓分：rich activities… explore the outdoors → 户外项目。community services（C）是她的志愿经历、teamwork（A）是她说自己的长处，都不是"club 最吸引她的点"。

**Q17. What would Brenda like to do for the club?　Brenda 想为社团做什么？　答案 A**
A. Plan activities. 策划活动　B. Train new members. 培训新成员　C. Look for volunteers. 招募志愿者
- 抓分：helping organize some club projects = 策划/组织活动。

#### 第 10 段（独白：番茄工作法）　对应 Q18–20

> **听力原文**
> Kevin has been using the Pomodoro Technique for a while.
> 凯文使用番茄工作法已经有一段时间了。
> Pomodoro means tomato in Italian.
> Pomodoro 在意大利语里是"番茄"的意思。
> This technique was created by a man named Francesco Cirillo in Italy.
> 这种方法是由一位名叫 Francesco Cirillo 的人在意大利创立的。
> The man was inspired by his mom's tomato-shaped kitchen timer.
> 他的灵感来自妈妈那只番茄形状的厨房计时器。
> That bright red timer ran softly while his mother was cooking, teaching him that short periods of work are more effective than long, tiring hours.
> 妈妈做饭时，那只鲜红的计时器轻轻地走着，让他懂得：短时段的工作比长时间、令人疲惫的工作更有效。
> Although this method is commonly used in offices and factories, Kevin applies it to his studies.
> 尽管这种方法常用于办公室和工厂，凯文却把它用在学习上。
> He begins by breaking his tasks into smaller ones, and then uses the timer to divide his time into focused work periods and short breaks.
> 他先把任务拆成更小的部分，再用计时器把时间分成专注工作时段和短暂休息。
> For example, when doing homework, Kevin lists the different stages of the task.
> 比如做作业时，凯文会列出任务的不同阶段。
> He then sets the timer for 25 minutes to work on the first task.
> 然后他把计时器设为 25 分钟，先做第一项任务。
> Once the timer rings, he takes a 10-minute break, using the time to move around and check off the completed task.
> 计时器一响，他就休息 10 分钟，趁机活动一下，并勾掉已完成的任务。
> After the break, he starts another 25-minute period.
> 休息后，他再开始另一个 25 分钟的时段。
> This method has helped Kevin complete his homework much faster than before.
> 这种方法帮助凯文比以前更快地完成作业。

**Q18. Who invented the Pomodoro Technique?　番茄工作法是谁发明的？　答案 C**
A. Kevin. 凯文　B. Kevin's mother. 凯文的妈妈　C. An Italian man. 一位意大利人
- 抓分：created by Francesco Cirillo in Italy = 意大利人。Kevin 只是**使用者**（A）；番茄计时器是 **Cirillo 妈妈**的，不是 Kevin 妈妈（B 张冠李戴）。

**Q19. How long does Kevin's normal work period last each time?　Kevin 每次正常的工作时段持续多久？　答案 C**
A. 10 minutes. 10 分钟　B. 20 minutes. 20 分钟　C. 25 minutes. 25 分钟
- 抓分：work period = **25 minutes**；10 分钟是**休息**（A 干扰），20（B）纯干扰。听数字先抓限定词（work period / break）。

**Q20. What is the Pomodoro Technique used for?　番茄工作法是用来做什么的？　答案 A**
A. Managing time well. 管理好时间　B. Solving study problems. 解决学习问题　C. Improving cooking skills. 提升厨艺
- 抓分：把时间切成"专注工作 + 短休息" → 本质是时间管理（A）。用于学习 → 偷换成 B；妈妈 cooking/番茄 → 诱导 C，都是无关细节。

## 四、语法点：`the + 形容词` 表"一类人"

本卷出现 **the elderly（老人）、the homeless（流浪者）、the sick（病人，第 15 题选项）**。
- 规则：`the + adj.` 指**一类人**，作主语时谓语动词用**复数**。
- 拓展：the rich / the poor / the young / the old / the deaf / the blind / the injured / the unemployed。
- 课堂翻译小练：
  1. 富人未必快乐。→ The rich **are** not always happy.
  2. 我们应当帮助老人和病人。→ We should help **the elderly and the sick**.

## 五、做题策略（开课前 5 分钟过一遍）

1. **预读**：圈疑问词（who / why / where / how long…）+ 选项关键词（数字、人名、地点、动词）。
2. **听音定位**：只抓疑问词对应的信息；听到选项**原词**往往是**错的**。
3. **本卷集中考的三类干扰**（带学生归纳）：
   - **转折否定**：Well / but / no sun → 转折后才是答案（Q1、Q4、Q8、Q12）
   - **张冠李戴**：把甲的信息安到乙头上（Q2、Q7、Q15、Q18）
   - **数字 / 常识诱导**：25 是番茄钟"常识"，但要听清问的是 work period 还是 break（Q19）
4. **数字题**：先抓限定词（normal / work period / break）再记数字。
5. **主旨题**（Q5、Q20）：答案常在**首句或尾句**，中间多是细节。
