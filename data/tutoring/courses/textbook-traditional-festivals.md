# 高考英语课文精讲：Traditional Festivals

> **课型**：课文精读 + 高考作文素材积累
> **适用对象**：高二 / 高三
> **建议时长**：40–45 分钟
> **配套音频**：详情页"音频资源"区可在线收听老师讲解版（女声慢速）

## 课文原文

Traditional festivals are significant occasions that reflect a nation's culture and beliefs. During the Spring Festival, people decorate their houses with lanterns and set off firecrackers to drive away evil spirits. Children dress up in fancy costumes, while families gather together in a joyful atmosphere.

What attracts me most is that festivals range from religious ceremonies to harvest celebrations, showing the origin of different customs. In some regions, people perform the samba or organize wrestling and archery competitions, which represent local traditions.

To be frank, although some festivals are becoming commercialized, they still remind us to express gratitude and respect to our family members. After all, festivals are not only a medium of entertainment but also a way to strengthen people's faith and inner joy.

---

# 逐句精讲

### ① Traditional festivals are significant occasions that reflect a nation’s culture and beliefs.

**翻译：**
传统节日是反映一个民族文化和信仰的重要庆典。

**语法分析：**

* Traditional festivals（主语）
* are（系动词）
* significant occasions（表语）
* that reflect a nation's culture and beliefs（定语从句修饰 occasions）

**高考考点：**

* 定语从句
* reflect = 反映；体现

---

### ② During the Spring Festival, people decorate their houses with lanterns and set off firecrackers to drive away evil spirits.

**翻译：**
春节期间，人们用灯笼装饰房屋，并燃放爆竹来驱赶邪恶的精灵。

**语法分析：**

* During the Spring Festival（时间状语）
* people（主语）
* decorate... and set off...（并列谓语）
* with lanterns（方式状语）
* to drive away evil spirits（目的状语）

**高考考点：**

* decorate A with B
* set off firecrackers
* 不定式作目的状语

---

### ③ Children dress up in fancy costumes, while families gather together in a joyful atmosphere.

**翻译：**
孩子们穿上华丽的服装，而家人们则在欢乐的氛围中团聚。

**语法分析：**

* Children（主语）
* dress up in fancy costumes（谓语）
* while（并列连词）
* families gather together（第二个分句）

**高考考点：**

* dress up in...
* gather together
* while表示“与此同时”

---

### ④ What attracts me most is that festivals range from religious ceremonies to harvest celebrations, showing the origin of different customs.

**翻译：**
最吸引我的是，节日涵盖从宗教仪式到丰收庆典等多种形式，展现了不同习俗的起源。

**语法分析：**

主干：

* What attracts me most（主语从句）
* is（系动词）
* that festivals range from...（表语从句）

showing the origin of different customs

= 现在分词短语作结果状语

**高考考点：**

* What引导主语从句
* that引导表语从句
* range from...to...
* 分词作状语

---

### ⑤ In some regions, people perform the samba or organize wrestling and archery competitions, which represent local traditions.

**翻译：**
在一些地区，人们会跳桑巴舞或举办摔跤和射箭比赛，这些活动代表着当地传统。

**语法分析：**

* In some regions（地点状语）
* people（主语）
* perform / organize（并列谓语）
* which represent local traditions（非限制性定语从句）

**高考考点：**

* 非限制性定语从句
* represent = 象征；代表

---

### ⑥ To be frank, although some festivals are becoming commercialized, they still remind us to express gratitude and respect to our family members.

**翻译：**
坦率地说，尽管一些节日正变得越来越商业化，但它们仍然提醒我们向家人表达感激与尊重。

**语法分析：**

主句：

* they still remind us

宾语补足语：

* to express gratitude and respect

让步状语从句：

* although some festivals are becoming commercialized

**高考考点：**

* To be frank
* although引导让步状语从句
* remind sb to do sth

---

### ⑦ After all, festivals are not only a medium of entertainment but also a way to strengthen people’s faith and inner joy.

**翻译：**
毕竟，节日不仅是一种娱乐方式，也是增强人们信念和内心喜悦的重要途径。

**语法分析：**

主干：

* festivals（主语）
* are（系动词）

表语：

* not only a medium of entertainment
* but also a way to strengthen...

其中：

* to strengthen... 为不定式后置定语修饰 way

**高考考点：**

* not only...but also...
* 不定式作后置定语

# 高考作文可直接套用的万能句

### ① 介绍节日意义

> Traditional festivals are significant occasions that reflect a nation’s culture and beliefs.

传统节日是反映民族文化和信仰的重要庆典。

### ② 描述节日活动

> People decorate their houses with lanterns and set off firecrackers to celebrate the festival.

人们挂灯笼、放鞭炮庆祝节日。

### ③ 描述文化价值

> Festivals remind us to express gratitude and respect to others.

节日提醒我们向他人表达感恩与尊重。

### ④ 升华主题

> Festivals are not only a way of entertainment but also a bridge connecting people with their traditions.

节日不仅是一种娱乐方式，更是连接人们与传统文化的桥梁。

# 文中出现的重要短语

## 1. decorate ... with ...

装饰……

例句：

> People decorate their houses with lanterns.

结构：

```text
decorate + 地点/物体 + with + 装饰物
```

高考作文常用：

> We decorated the classroom with colorful balloons.

---

## 2. set off firecrackers

燃放鞭炮

例句：

> People set off firecrackers.

注意：

set off 有多个意思：

| 意思  | 例句                             |
| --- | ------------------------------ |
| 出发  | We set off early.              |
| 引起  | The news set off a discussion. |
| 使爆炸 | set off fireworks              |

---

## 3. dress up in

穿上盛装

例句：

> Children dress up in fancy costumes.

结构：

```text
dress up in + 衣服
```

高考常考：

> The students dressed up in traditional Chinese clothes.

---

## 4. range from ... to ...

范围从……到……

例句：

> Festivals range from religious ceremonies to harvest celebrations.

结构：

```text
range from A to B
```

高级表达：

> The topics range from education to environmental protection.

---

## 5. gather together

团聚

例句：

> Families gather together.

高考节日作文高频：

> Family members gather together to enjoy a big dinner.

---

## 6. express gratitude

表达感激

例句：

> express gratitude and respect

高级搭配：

| 搭配                      | 意思      |
| ----------------------- | ------- |
| express gratitude to sb | 向某人表达感谢 |
| show gratitude          | 表达感谢    |
| with gratitude          | 怀着感激之情  |

---

## 7. not only ... but also ...

不仅……而且……

例句：

> Festivals are not only a medium of entertainment but also a way to strengthen people’s faith.

高考满分句型：

```text
not only A but also B
```

例如：

> Reading can not only broaden our horizons but also enrich our minds.
